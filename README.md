## What I'm Building Toward

Education is often a guidance problem before it is a content problem. Students do not only need more resources; they need the right support at the right moment.

That is the lens behind my work: using **LLMs**, **retrieval-augmented generation**, and **agentic workflows** as practical infrastructure for research support, writing guidance, feedback, and personalized learning.
